Vijay Sethupathi Songs

Vijay Gurunatha Sethupathi (born 16 January 1978) is an Indian actor and film producer who predominantly works in Tamil films. He is a recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award, two Filmfare Awards South and two Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.
