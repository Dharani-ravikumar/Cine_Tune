Don

The movie Don is a Bollywood action thriller film released on April 20, 1978. It is an Indian Hindi-language film directed by Chandra Barot. The film features Amitabh Bachchan in the lead role, playing a dual character as both a crime lord and a common man impersonating him. The movie is known for its gripping plot, stylish direction, and unforgettable music composed by Kalyanji-Anandji.
