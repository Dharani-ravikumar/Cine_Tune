Master

Master is a Tamil-language action-thriller film that was released on January 13, 2021. The movie is from India and is directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. It stars Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi in leading roles and revolves around a young professor with a troubled past who takes a teaching job at a juvenile school, where he comes across a dangerous gangster. The film was noted for its high-octane action sequences and intense performances from the lead actors.
