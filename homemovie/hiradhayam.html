Hridayam

Hridayam is a Malayalam-language romantic drama film directed by Vineeth Sreenivasan. It was released on January 21, 2022, in India. The film follows the life journey of Arun Neelakandan, a young man navigating love, friendships, and personal growth from his college days to adulthood. The story is both nostalgic and heartwarming, exploring the highs and lows of Arun's journey with a blend of humor and emotion.
